ALT record, resources

Last updated: April 24, 2023

Hello! If you've poked around my main website, you might know that I've been working as an Assistant Language Teacher (ALT) in Japan for the past couple years. This website is here to serve as a collection of resources and information based on my experiences at a local junior high school.

School year overview

April events

  • First week Teacher welcoming ceremony: Introducing new teachers to the second/third years
  • First week Opening ceremony: Right after the teacher welcoming ceremony, the school year begins for second/third years
  • First week Entrance ceremony: First day of JHS for first years. Parents and guardians come to celebrate!
  • 29 Showa Day: The first day Golden Week..ish

May events

  • 3 Constitutional Memorial Day
  • 4 Greenery Day
  • 5 Children's Day
  • Mid-late May School trips: The second and third years go on overnight trips! First years have a day excursion in the city.

June events

  • The first round of semester exams is in June.

July events

  • Sports days! The school I work at has separate days for each school year.
  • And of course, after the closing ceremony..summer break begins!

August events

  • 11 Mountain Day
  • ...and back to school where I will say something weird in Japanese and spend the rest of the day thinking about it.

September events

  • Semester exams part two...
  • ...and the long-awaited school festival!

October events

  • For the first time since I came to Japan, we will have open school days and a choral competition!
